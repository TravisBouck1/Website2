The Applied Experimental Research (AER) Lab at Cal Poly focuses on fluid mechanics and flow visualization through cutting-edge experiments. Our work spans aerodynamics, vortex dynamics, and measurement techniques like SPIV and PIV.

Our students gain hands-on experience with equipment and contribute to high-impact research published in international venues.
